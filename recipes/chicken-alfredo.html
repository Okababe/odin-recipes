Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken alfredo is a simple dish, made quite easily with a few ingredients.

If you decide to cook this dish, keep in mind that
not many people are fond of pasta these days..

Ingredients

  • Chicken, cooked and cut
  • Pasta of your choice
  • Alfredo sauce (pick a good one!)
  • Your favorite seasonings

Steps Taken

  1. Cook and cut your chicken, seasoned to taste
  2. Boil your pasta of choice, make sure its fully soft!
  3. Add your pasta to your alfredo sauce in a pan
  4. toss in your chicken and seasonings, then enjoy!
