Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

Honey Glazed Chicken is a very simple dish, one that many are fond of.
The simple combination of honey and chicken is something everyone can enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Chicken, cooked and cut
  • Honey, spicy is an option too
  • lime
  • Your favorite seasonings

Steps Taken

  1. Cook and cut your chicken, seasoned to taste
  2. Toss in some honey!
  3. season and dress with lime
